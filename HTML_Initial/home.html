Welcome to CSF.com

We are a club at the University of Central Florida (UCF).
CSF is a student run organization created to provide opportunities to develop industry demanded skills (& their resume) through the design, development, and operation of space-based technologies. We specialize in SmallSats and are compiling teams to work on more diverse projects like rovers. Our missions streamline members into research opportunities, gets them involved in senior design projects, exposes them to a space-based engineering environment, and submerges them with students inspired to learn.

We current have various projects being developed:

  1. 2 DIY CubeSats that will be launched on a weather balloon to study atmoshperic chemistry and train our engineers in SmallSat development.
  2. NASA Lunar Torch Design Competition: Rover subsystem design to redirect light into lunar tunnels and craters as well as heat-up cold electronics.
  3. NASA BIG Idea Challenge: designing lunar rover with non-traditional mobility systems (no-wheels).
  4. University Student Design Challenge: A design competition hosted by NASA Glen. Details will be released in the Fall semester.
  5. PlantSat: a senior-design project sponsored by NASA KSC focused on developing growing food in orbit without human interaction.
